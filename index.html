About the School

Shaped by Purpose,
Built for Impact

The School of Computer Studies at USJ-R offers rigorous technology education within a tradition of Augustinian-Recollect formation — developing not just competent technologists, but ethical ones ready for a complex digital world.

Practical Curriculum Industry-aligned programs built around current tools, real systems, and hands-on projects.
Multiple Pathways From associate to graduate — a program for every level of ambition and career goal.
Faculty-Led Excellence Educators who bridge academic rigor with lived industry experience and research.
